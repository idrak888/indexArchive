What is an Extrovert and an Introvert?

Most of you might be wondering what Extrovert and Introvert really means and which one am I?
Well the answer isn't quite that simple.
An Extrovert is a person who is simply very outgoing, socially active and confident.

Whereas, an Introvert is a type of person who is mostly just shy and recitent.
You know you are an introvert when you don't really like crowds and you would rather stay
home and watch TV alone.

How do I know which one I am?

It is pretty obvious that you are an extrovert if you like going out and socializing alot.
Not just that, the amount of friends you have and how much you actually talk to them can
say alot about you too.

If thats you, you know you are most probably an extrovert.

Unlike extroverts, though some introverts may like going out, they are mostly shy
and will rarely even speak.
Introverts tend to be inward turning as many say and will have their brains filled
with personal thoughts.

Well yeah, that explains most of it.

At the end of the day, none of these personalities are to be judged and
being yourself wins!

I hope that helped but if you are still curious and are still not sure
which one you are, don't be sad, take this simple test and find out your true self!
